Make your dream baseball team

The chart shows performance of baseball players using their Home Runs (HR) and average scores (avg). Make a strong team by selecting hitters from top 5% players. Decide players based on their batting orientation and the trade off between high average scores and home runs.

The 95% quantile Home Runs for all players is 200 and avergage score is 0.28 .The top right area have players with high average and Home runs. The 95% quantiles for both home runs (219) and average scores (0.2875) for Left-handed players is highest .There are many left-handed top performers even there are less of player with such handedness in total. The probable reason is that pitchers are not comfortable with such players. The 95% quantile home for right-handed players is approximately same as that of all players while they have lower 95% quantile average scores. There are more right-handed players in the top right area which belongs to players with high home runs and average scores. Both-handed players are less in number and have lowest 95% quantile home runs at 150. Overall, it can be said that Left-handed players are top performers and would be the best bet to choose from all players for your team.

The visualization was created using data set containing 1,157 baseball players including their handedness (right or left handed), height (in inches), weight (in pounds), batting average, and home runs.
